Price

All prices of products and services on this website are in Australian Dollar (AUD) and inclusive of GST (Good and Service Tax).

We reserve the right to change the prices without notice, however if it happens after you have placed your order we will contact you prior to delivery.

We accept payment by credit card Mastercard or Visa, and the host of our website provide us with very secure online shopping through PAYPAL. The transaction will not be processed until the goods are ready to be sent.

However, the option of paying by bank deposit, or by check is also available, and we will provide you with bank details and postal address upon request. We will just wait for payment to be cleared before sending the merchandise.
