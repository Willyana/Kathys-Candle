Payment

Credit Cards

We currently accept Visa & MasterCard. Please note that our secure real-time credit card processing facilities will charge your card at the time the order is placed. The charge will appear on your statement as "Kathy's Company".

Paying online is quick, easy and secure - just choose the "pay by credit card" option at the checkout, then follow the step by step instructions. (It's completely safe - read more about our security policy)

Alternatively you can phone or fax your credit card details through to our head office. Simply call our Website Customer Service on 02 9531 1244 or Fax 02 9531 1266. Please include your name, phone & order number on the fax.

Paypal

PayPal is a quick, convenient and secure way of sending and receiving money online.

Getting started with PayPal is very quick and easy. It only takes a few minutes to get setup and then you can purchase immediately through PayPal using your credit card. Click on the "Paypal " button on the order confirmation page after completing and submitting your shipping details. Alternatively, go to the www.paypal.com.au website and log in (or sign up if you don't already have a Paypal account).

To learn more about PayPal or sign up, click here:www.paypal.com.au.
