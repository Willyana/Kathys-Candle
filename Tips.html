Candle's care

The best way to use candles...

Trim the wick to about 5mm before each time you burn your candle. This will reduce smoking and ensure proper burning.

The first time you burn your candle, burn it until the entire surface is melted, usually at least two hours. Soy wax has a 'memory' and if this isn't done, the candle won't burn evenly the next time.

Store your candles in a cool and dark place. Direct sunlight can effect the colour of the candle.

Keep debris from falling into the wax as this will pollute the wax and can lead to an uneven burn.

<

Safety...

Ensure you only burn candles on a heatproof surface.

Never leave a candle (burning or not) in reach of small children

Burn your candle on a flat and stable surface away from draughts.

Never let your candle burn unattended! This is a major cause of house fires.

Keep lighting instruments away and out of reach of children.

Melts...

Use a good quality, Australian approved electric warmer or tea light warmer only.

Always remove packaging before placing melts in the burner.

Never leave the burner un attended.

Keep away from kids & pets.

Don't let the wax get too low in the burner, remember to top it up regularly.

Always follow the safety instructions that came with your Burner and Melts.
