Delivery & Returns

Delivery

All orders from Kathy's Candles are processed between 9am and 3:30pm, Monday through Friday. As long as we receive your order within our processing time and the items that you order are in stock, your order will usually be shipped within 24 hours.

Once our items are shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with the tracking information after the product has left our warehouse.

We do not ship on holidays. All orders $200.00 or more will be shipped "Signature Required"

Free Shipping (promo only) Offers

For Free Shipping, you must select the "Free Shipping (promo only) " option at checkout. Standard promo shipping charges will be deducted from your order when you make a purchase for the amount required by the promotion.

All products must be shipped to the same address per order. We process orders Monday through Friday in the order in which they are received. All orders are usually processed by the next business day.

Return Policy

Excluding your consumer rights under ACL, Our policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging. We do not offer returns on custom made orders.

To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase. Please do not send your purchase back to us without consulting us first.

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.

Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at Request &Enquiry.
